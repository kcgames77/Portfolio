About Me

Hello there! I’m a gameplay programmer and developer with a focus on systems that feel responsive, fluid, and fun. My background blends Computer Science fundamentals with hands-on experience designing and coding full gameplay loops from prototype to the polished build.

I’ve built action platformers, traversal systems, AI behavior trees and finite state machines, as well as physics-driven mechanics using GameMaker Studio 2 and Unreal Engine 5. My goal is always the same: to make interactions feel so smooth players forget they’re just inputs and math.

A part from making and playing video games, I love cooking and trying out new dishes. I have a knack of experiencing new flavors and learning different recipes. I also enjoy meeting new people as well as exploring different cultures. During my pasttime, I love discussing and reading philosophy books.
